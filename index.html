Note on Version 1 ("As Is"): The quote block (bar and text) is indented from the preceding text.

Note on Version 2 ("Hanging"): A .text-column-container creates a defined text column with a left gutter (padding). Both the question text and the quote's text align to the start of this column. The quote's vertical bar (::before pseudo-element) is then positioned to "hang" into that left gutter, to the left of the aligned text. This demonstrates the principle of aligning by the text edge and letting other elements (like quotation marks or decorative bars) hang.
